FAQ & WebAR Troubleshooting

Having trouble viewing our furniture in your space? Follow these quick steps:

  • Camera Permissions: Ensure your browser (Chrome/Safari) has permission to access your device's camera.
  • Target Marker: You must point your camera at the correct 'Hiro' target marker. Ensure the marker is printed clearly or displayed fully on another bright screen.
  • Lighting: WebAR works best in well-lit environments. Avoid harsh shadows across the target marker.
  • Distance: Hold your camera roughly 30cm to 50cm away from the marker to allow the 3D model to anchor properly.
